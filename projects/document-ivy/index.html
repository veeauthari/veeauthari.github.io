Stan Twitter and Culture

Interviewer: Ivy Kurniawan

Interviewee: Ali / @thecruelcities

Twitter is a platform that is unlike most other popular social media platforms. The way that the website/app has been constructed inevitably creates a very social, interactive, and public environment. Unlike Instagram, Facebook, and Snapchat, many Twitter users have no real-life contacts following their account. In fact, they try to hide their accounts from their friends and family, because they use it as a place to be candid and to express themselves freely. Here, Ali describes what compelled her to start Twitter in the first place.
